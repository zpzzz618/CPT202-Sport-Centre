Swimming Court

The swimming pool facility is situated within the Sports Centre and spans across 1200 square meters.
It boasts a length of 50 meters and a width of 25 meters, providing ample space for various swimming activities.
The pool is divided into six lanes, each designed for different swimming speeds and techniques.
In the deep end, the water depth reaches 3 meters, while the shallow end maintains a depth of 1.2 meters.
The water temperature is carefully maintained at a comfortable 28°C, ensuring an enjoyable swimming experience for all patrons.
